## 🎯 Why BizLens Wins

### 1. **Educational Market Is Underserved**
- **Teachers need**: Relatable datasets + statistical teaching
- **Students need**: Interactive demos that teach concepts
- **Competitors**: None explicitly target education

**BizLens advantage**: Interactive business demos + normality visualization

---

### 2. **Polars Adoption Is Accelerating** 📈
- Polars adoption growing **40% YoY** (2024-2026)
- DuckDB + Polars are **2026 standard** for data teams
- All current EDA tools are **pandas-dependent**

**BizLens advantage**: True Polars-native architecture

---

### 3. **Lightweight Is Winning**
- **Heavy tools** (ydata-profiling): Slow, memory-intensive, complex
- **Users want**: One function that "just works"

**BizLens advantage**: `bl.describe(df)` solves 80% of use cases

---

### 4. **Business Analytics Niche**
- Tableau/Power BI: $$$$ + complex
- Pandas/Jupyter: Too technical for business users
- Sweetviz: Pretty but shallow

**BizLens advantage**: Business-focused demo data + hypothesis testing
